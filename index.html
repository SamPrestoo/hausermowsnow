About Us

Hauser Mow & Snow LLC was founded by brothers Mikael Hauser (18) and Dominik Hauser (17) with the goal of saving money for education. We service lawns in the West Madison and Middleton area and are always looking to improve and expand.

Mikael is 18 years old, he enjoys working out, snowboarding and traveling. He runs all the Business side of the company. Dominik is the younger brother to Mikael at 17 years old. He enjoys playing soccer and working on engines. Dominik handles all equipment mechanics and maintenance.
