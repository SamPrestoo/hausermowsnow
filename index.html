About Us

Hauser Mow & Snow LLC was founded by brothers Mikael Hauser (18) and Dominik Hauser (17) with the goal of saving money for their education. Our company specializes in providing exceptional lawn care services in Madison, WI, particularly focusing on the West Madison and Middleton areas. Always striving to improve and expand, we ensure your lawns receive the best care possible.

Mikael, who is 18 years old, enjoys working out, snowboarding, and traveling. He efficiently manages all the business aspects of our company. Dominik, the younger brother at 17 years old, has a passion for playing soccer and working on engines. He is responsible for all equipment mechanics and maintenance. Together, they combine their skills and interests to deliver top-quality lawn care services. We also proudly extend our services to nearby cities such as Fitchburg, Verona, and Sun Prairie. Choose Hauser Mow & Snow LLC for all your lawncare needs and see the difference professional care can make!

Lawn Care Services

We offer a full range of lawn care services to ensure your yard looks its best. Our services include:

  • Lawn Mowing: Regular mowing services to keep your grass at the perfect height.
  • Fertilization: Custom fertilization plans to promote healthy, lush lawns.
  • Weed Control: Effective weed management to keep your lawn weed-free.
  • Aeration: Improve soil health and lawn vitality with our aeration services.
  • Dethatching: Remove thatch build-up to enhance grass growth and overall lawn health.
